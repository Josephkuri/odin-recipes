Banana Pudding Recipe

Indulge in the creamy nostalgia of homemade banana pudding with this comforting recipe. Layers of ripe bananas, luscious vanilla pudding, and delicate vanilla wafers come together in a dessert that's both timeless and irresistible.

Whether you're preparing it for a family gathering or simply craving a sweet treat, each spoonful promises a symphony of flavors and textures that will leave everyone asking for seconds.

Perfectly chilled and topped with a crown of whipped cream or meringue, this banana pudding embodies the essence of homemade goodness

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 cups cold skim milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • Â½ (12 ounce) package vanilla wafers
  • 4 bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat cream cheese in a large bowl until fluffy. Beat in condensed milk, then pudding mix. Gradually mix in cold milk until smooth, followed by vanilla.
  3. Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping.
  4. Line the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish with vanilla wafers.
  5. Arrange sliced bananas evenly on top.
  6. Cover with pudding mixture.
  7. Top with remaining whipped topping.
  8. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours before serving.
